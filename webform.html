Featured Speaker: Michael Facemire

FORRESTER RESEARCH, INC. VP AND PRINCIPAL ANALYST.

Topic: A Blast Through The Future of Mobile

It's been 10 years since Apple introduced the iPhone, and kicked off the current wave of mobile experiences. So what do we expect from mobile during the next 10 years? How will experiences move from a pure app and web world of today to a blended ecosystem of experiences tomorrow. Take a journey through the future of mobile, and learn about what technologies we use today will power the experiences of tomorrow!
