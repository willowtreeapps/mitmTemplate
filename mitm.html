Topic: A Blast Through The Future of Mobile

It's been 10 years since Apple introduced the iPhone, and kicked off the current wave of mobile experiences. So what do we expect from mobile during the next 10 years? How will experiences move from a pure app and web world of today to a blended ecosystem of experiences tomorrow. Take a journey through the future of mobile, and learn about what technologies we use today will power the experiences of tomorrow!

What we’ll cover:

  • Next Gen Technologies, including bots, multi-modal, interactive TV, and VR/AR
  • App Distribution and Audience Development, including app indexing, deep linking and instant apps
  • Monetization and Payments, including native ad and analytics
  • Engagement through UX, notifications, and AI
  • Security, including recent trends of attorneys general going after large companies with mobile security holes, and predictions that a Fortune 1000 company will fail due to a cyberbreach in 2017
  • Best Practices in Mobile Development, including emerging platforms such as React and Xamarin, as well as automation of QA
